## Review Collection Strategy

### Goal: 10-15 reviews in first 3 months

**Method 1: Direct Request (Easiest)**
- Email customers: "We'd love your feedback on Google"
- Include direct link to Google Business Profile
- Post-purchase: Request reviews in receipt/delivery

**Method 2: QR Code**
- Create QR code linking to Google review page
- Display in shop, on receipts, in delivery packaging
- Simple instruction: "Scan to leave a review"

**Method 3: SMS/WhatsApp**
- Send review request via messaging after event (Partyservice)
- Include direct link
- Personal touch: "Marco here from Metzgerei Müller..."

**Method 4: Staff Training**
- Empower team to ask for reviews in person
- Offer simple "We'd appreciate your feedback" conversation
- Make it part of customer service routine
